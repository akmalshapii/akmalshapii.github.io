Me

About

I'm a Data Engineer based in Malaysia, mainly working on building cloud-native data pipelines on Google Cloud Platform. I started my career in 2021 with no prior industry experience — and have spent the years since learning by doing real production systems.

I work across the full data pipeline lifecycle — from ingestion and transformation to orchestration and delivery. My work typically feeds Data Scientists and Analysts, so I think a lot about data quality, reliability, and making sure the right data is available at the right time.

Outside of pipelines, I'm drawn to the intersection of data and AI — particularly how large language models can be embedded into data workflows to add real, practical value.
