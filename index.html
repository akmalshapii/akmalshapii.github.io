Me

About

I'm a Data Engineer at REV Media Group, one of Malaysia's leading digital media companies. I joined fresh out of university in June 2021 with no prior industry experience — and have been building production data systems on Google Cloud Platform ever since.

My day-to-day revolves around designing and maintaining ETL/ELT workflows using Cloud Composer (Apache Airflow), working with BigQuery for large-scale analytics, and integrating AI/ML capabilities via Vertex AI and Gemini APIs.

I care deeply about building systems that are reliable, cost-efficient, and genuinely useful — whether the consumer is a Data Scientist, a Data Analyst, or a product team shipping a feature.
